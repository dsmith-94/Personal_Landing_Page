# Personal Landing Page

## Overview

This project is a personal professional landing page built to showcase my background, technical skills, and data analytics projects. It is designed to provide a clean, accessible way for recruiters, hiring managers, and technical peers to quickly evaluate my experience and explore my work in more detail.

The site complements my resume and LinkedIn by highlighting real projects, tools, and outcomes in a structured and easy-to-navigate format.

## About Me

I am a Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa (Tippie College of Business), graduating in May 2026. I am pursuing opportunities in data analytics and related fields where I can apply data-driven problem solving to real business challenges.

## Features

* Clean, responsive single-page layout
* Clearly structured sections: Hero, About, Skills, Projects, Contact
* Highlighted data analytics projects with tools and outcomes
* Easy navigation with smooth scrolling
* Professional presentation focused on both technical and business skills

## Technologies Used

* HTML5 (semantic structure)
* CSS3 (custom styling, responsive design)
* JavaScript (basic interactivity and smooth scrolling)
