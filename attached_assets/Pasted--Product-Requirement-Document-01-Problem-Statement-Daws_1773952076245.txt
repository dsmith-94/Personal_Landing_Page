# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Dawson Smith, a Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa (graduating May 2026), wants a personal landing page to **differentiate himself from other candidates** and provide a **clean, accessible way for recruiters and peers to evaluate his work and technical ability**. The page will give a **high-level view of experience with the option to explore projects in depth**, going beyond what a resume or LinkedIn profile can convey. This curated professional presence emphasizes both business and technical skills, highlighting real-world projects, internship experience, and analytical capabilities.

## 02 — Target Audience

**Primary audience:**

* HR representatives and hiring managers recruiting for data analyst / data-focused roles at medium to large corporate or consulting firms in the Midwest.

**Secondary audience:**

* Data analysts and technical team members evaluating technical skills.
* Broader U.S.-based opportunities beyond the Midwest.

**Visitor goals:**

* Quickly understand **who Dawson is**, his professional background, and relevant work experience.
* Gain confidence in his ability to **apply technical skills to real business problems**.
* Access **detailed project examples** to explore deeper if interested.

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal                                          | How We Measure It                             |
| --------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Generate recruiter or hiring manager contacts | 5–10 contacts within 30–60 days               |
| Secure interviews                             | 2–3 interview opportunities within 30–60 days |
| Understand site visibility                    | Track number of site visits                   |
